CITU Library Map


The Library Map aims in teaching you where various section of the library can be found as well as define each sections in the libarry.

Technology Section


Contain various programming book in languges such as python, C/C++, java, etc.

Periodicals Section


Journals currently subscribed and journals received as gift are displayed in the shelves in this section. News papers and popular magazines are also available for reading within the section.

Reference Section


This is the place to go to find dictionaries, encyclopedias, almanacs, bibliographies and other books needed to look up when specific information is needed such as eographical, historical, political and economic maps and atlases.
